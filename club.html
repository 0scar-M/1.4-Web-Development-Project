About

━━

The Mountain Turk Club was founded in 2019 and has been building and maintaining the Mahu Whenua Traverse ever since. Membership is either $300 or 10 hours at a working bee helping to build a Turk. As of now, there aren't any more Turks being built and consequently no working bees to go to, but hopefully there will be sometime in the future.

Thank you to the Mountain Turk Club and Eric Bradshaw for building and maintaining this world-class alpine route.
